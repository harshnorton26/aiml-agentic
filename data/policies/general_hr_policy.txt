# HR Policy Document - General HR Guidelines

## General HR Policies and Guidelines

### Working Hours
- **Standard Hours**: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (with 1 hour lunch break).
- **Core Hours**: All employees must be available from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
- **Flexible Timing**: Employees may start between 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM with manager approval.
- **Total Hours**: 40 hours per week is the standard expectation.

### Public Holidays
- **Annual Holidays**: The company observes 15 public holidays per year.
- **Holiday List**: Published at the beginning of each calendar year.
- **Location-Based**: Holidays may vary based on office location and local observances.
- **Substitute Holidays**: If a holiday falls on a weekend, a substitute day off will be announced.

### Attendance and Punctuality
- **Attendance Tracking**: Logged through biometric system or HR portal.
- **Late Arrival**: Frequent late arrivals (more than 3 times per month) will be flagged.
- **Early Departure**: Must be approved by manager in advance.
- **Monthly Reports**: Attendance reports are reviewed monthly by HR and managers.

### Code of Conduct
- Maintain professional behavior and respect towards colleagues.
- Dress code is business casual; smart casual on Fridays.
- Confidential company information must not be shared externally.
- Use of company resources for personal purposes should be minimal.

### Performance Reviews
- **Frequency**: Conducted bi-annually (every 6 months).
- **Components**: Self-assessment, manager review, and peer feedback.
- **Outcomes**: May result in promotions, salary adjustments, or development plans.
- **Timeline**: January-February and July-August review cycles.

### Probation Period
- **Duration**: 3 months for all new joiners.
- **Purpose**: To evaluate job fit, performance, and cultural alignment.
- **Evaluation**: Manager will conduct a formal review at the end of probation.
- **Confirmation**: Employee must be formally confirmed after successful completion.
- **Extension**: Probation may be extended by 30-60 days if needed for assessment.
- **Early Termination**: Either party can terminate with reduced notice during probation period.

### Grievance Redressal
- Employees can raise concerns with HR or use the anonymous feedback portal.
- All grievances will be investigated confidentially.
- Response timeline: HR will acknowledge within 3 working days.
- Resolution expected within 15 working days for standard cases.

### Training and Development
- **Annual Budget**: Each employee has access to learning and development budget.
- **Internal Training**: Regular workshops and skill development sessions.
- **External Training**: May be sponsored with manager and HR approval.
- **Certifications**: Company may sponsor relevant professional certifications.

### Resignation and Notice Period
- **Notice Period**: 30 days for junior positions, 60 days for senior positions.
- **Exit Interview**: Conducted by HR to gather feedback.
- **Clearance**: Must return all company property and complete exit formalities.
- **Early Release**: Possible with approval if replacement is found.

### Employee Benefits
- Health insurance coverage for employee and dependents.
- Annual health check-up sponsored by the company.
- Gym membership reimbursement (up to 50%).
- Employee referral bonus program.
- Meal vouchers or subsidized cafeteria.
