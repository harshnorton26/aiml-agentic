# HR Policy Document - Leave Policy

## Company Leave Policy

### Annual Leave
- **Entitlement**: All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per year.
- **Accrual**: Leave accrues at 1.67 days per month.
- **Carryforward**: Up to 5 unused annual leave days may be carried forward to the next calendar year.
- **Expiry**: Any leave beyond 5 days that is not taken will expire on December 31st.
- **Application**: Annual leave requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance for approval.

### Sick Leave
- **Entitlement**: 12 days of sick leave per year for all full-time employees.
- **Medical Certificate**: Required for absences of 3 or more consecutive days.
- **Notification**: Employees must notify their manager by 9 AM on the day of absence.
- **Unused**: Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the next year.
- **Emergency**: In case of emergency hospitalization, medical documentation must be submitted within 3 days of return.

### Casual Leave
- **Entitlement**: 10 days of casual leave per year.
- **Purpose**: For personal emergencies, family events, or urgent matters.
- **Notice**: Should be applied at least 24 hours in advance when possible.
- **Medical Certificate**: Not required for casual leave.
- **Carryforward**: Cannot be carried forward to the next year.

### Maternity Leave
- **Entitlement**: Female employees are entitled to 180 days (6 months) of paid maternity leave.
- **Eligibility**: Must have completed 6 months of service with the company.
- **Application**: Should be applied at least 4 weeks before the expected delivery date.
- **Extension**: Can be extended by 30 days with medical certification.

### Paternity Leave
- **Entitlement**: Male employees are entitled to 15 days of paid paternity leave.
- **Eligibility**: Available upon the birth or adoption of a child.
- **Application**: Should be applied within 2 weeks of the child's birth.
- **Timing**: Must be taken within 3 months of the child's birth.

### Bereavement Leave
- **Immediate Family**: 5 days of paid leave for the death of an immediate family member (spouse, parent, child, sibling).
- **Extended Family**: 3 days for grandparents, in-laws, or other close relatives.
- **Documentation**: Death certificate may be requested.
- **No advance notice required**: Due to the nature of bereavement.

### Compensatory Off (Comp Off)
- **Eligibility**: Granted for working on designated company holidays or weekends.
- **Approval**: Must be pre-approved by manager before working on the holiday.
- **Validity**: Comp off must be utilized within 30 days of earning it.
- **Application**: Can be applied with 1 day notice.

### Leave Without Pay (LWP)
- **Availability**: When all paid leave is exhausted.
- **Approval**: Requires manager and HR approval.
- **Impact**: May affect salary, benefits, and annual performance evaluation.
- **Maximum**: Should not exceed 30 days in a calendar year without special approval.
