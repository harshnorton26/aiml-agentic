# HR Policy Document - Work From Home Policy

## Work From Home (WFH) Policy

### Overview
Our company supports a hybrid work model that allows employees to work from home while maintaining productivity and collaboration.

### Eligibility
- All full-time employees who have completed their probation period (3 months).
- Role must be suitable for remote work (as determined by manager).
- Must have a suitable home workspace with reliable internet connectivity.

### WFH Days
- **Standard Policy**: Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week.
- **Flexible Arrangement**: Additional WFH days may be approved on a case-by-case basis.
- **Team Coordination**: Teams should ensure adequate office presence for collaboration.

### Application Process
- WFH requests should be submitted through the HR portal at least 24 hours in advance.
- Recurring WFH schedules (e.g., every Monday and Friday) can be set up with manager approval.
- Ad-hoc WFH requests for emergencies can be approved via email or messaging.

### Requirements
- **Availability**: Must be available during core working hours (10 AM - 4 PM).
- **Communication**: Should respond to emails and messages within 2 hours during working hours.
- **Meetings**: Must attend all scheduled video/phone meetings.
- **Deliverables**: Work output and quality should match office performance.

### Equipment
- Company laptop and necessary equipment will be provided.
- Employees are responsible for maintaining a safe and secure workspace.
- IT support is available for technical issues during working hours.

### Restrictions
- WFH is not a substitute for child care or elder care.
- Employees working from home must be in a location with reliable internet.
- International WFH requires special approval due to tax and legal implications.

### Monitoring
- Performance will be evaluated based on deliverables and outcomes.
- Regular check-ins with managers to ensure alignment.
- WFH privilege may be revoked if performance or availability issues arise.
